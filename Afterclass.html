The Aga Khan Academy Hyderabad

The Aga Khan Academy Hyderabad is a prestigious educational institution located in Hyderabad, India. It is part of the Aga Khan Development Network and offers a rigorous International Baccalaureate (IB) curriculum to students from diverse backgrounds. The academy focuses on holistic education, fostering critical thinking, creativity, and leadership skills among its students. With state-of-the-art facilities and a commitment to academic excellence, the Aga Khan Academy Hyderabad aims to nurture future leaders who can contribute positively to society.


The total number of students in the academy is around 600. The academy provides education from primary to secondary levels, catering to students aged 4 to 18 years. It offers the Primary Years Programme (PYP), Middle Years Programme (MYP), and the Diploma Programme (DP) of the International Baccalaureate (IB) curriculum.
